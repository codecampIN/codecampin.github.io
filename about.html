About us

We are a bunch of young guys, some of us happen to be college droupouts and we know whats broken with current higher technical education, we want to fix that.



CodeCamp aims to help young people, specially college students, get practical, industry-relevant skill. We want to help them realize their potential, that they can learn and BUILD whatever they want and that education is really more than writing exam papers. We believe there is enough free quality resources available to learn things online but they need to be curated, structured and presented in effective manner, along with personal guidance to really maximize their benefit.



CodeCamp is just the begining.
